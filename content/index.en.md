Everything is my fault.
In this place, constructed by me, not only is every wrong thing mine, but every right thing is mine too.
[[jag.en|I]] write here. About whatever comes to mind. I work with an open door, without the slightest thought of virality or algorithm-adapted content.

The page is structured as a [[varfor-digital-garden.en|digital garden]], a collection of notes, essays, and ideas in various stages of maturity, linked by hyperlinks – a mysterious mutation of a blog and a wiki. Completely freed from chronology.

But what does a mythomaniac in a lie detector factory mean? I write a little about it [[premiss.en|here]].
