In the now-imaginary series of people I like, I'm starting with the aforementioned comedian. But who is Anthony Jeselnik, you might ask? He is a comedian perhaps best known for his at times dark jokes. And before you wave that off, I just want to say that's not why I like him. Yes, sure, he's funny. But here's a short list of why I respect him:
- His TikTok feed is occupied by his book club, which you can join by sending him an email. You read one book a month.
- Despite his brusque jokes, he has several times taken a stand against comedians who engage in "bullying," especially on the right wing.
- He comes across as an incredibly honest person.
I could be wrong. It might all just be fake. But that's how it feels to me.
