Instead of insidious flute playing, AI services seduce us with a cheesy 80s saxophone solo. Even I, as a diligent user of AI, realize that something is not as it should be. There is something wrong, someone who doesn't see that the emperor has no clothes and that he's playing the saxophone. All the talk about productivity, all the talk about intelligence. I both see it and don't. If it weren't for all the confusing spells channeled through [[businesschmodels|affärsmodellerna]], maybe we could distinguish what is what.

While waiting for clarity, I hesitantly dance towards the brink of ruin.
