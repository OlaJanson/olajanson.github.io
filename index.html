THE ATTENTION EXCHANGE

MY PROJECTS

INFERNAL VENTURES

Special forces colonizing hell for profit-hungry industrialists.

An alternate history early 1900s where you play mercenaries working for industrialists who decided to colonize hell. Heart of Darkness dressed as Indiana Jones. A card-based TTRPG about colonialism, capitalism, and the occult.

LAST DAYS OF EUROPE

A dying underwater colony on Jupiter's moon Europa.

You are people with great responsibility in a society on the brink of collapse. A claustrophobic sci-fi drama about human relationships and mysteries in an isolated, baroque-decorated colony beneath the ice.

BLACK METAL DETECTIVE AGENCY

Stand by Me meets True Detective.

Kids in a black metal band solving crimes the authorities don't understand because they don't understand the subculture.

WHY NOTHING GETS DONE

I get excited about ideas. I start them. Then life happens, or I get distracted by the next shiny thing. This site is my attempt to actually finish and share.
